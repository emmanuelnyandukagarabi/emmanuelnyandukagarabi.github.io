Master's Thesis

AI-Powered Classification and Early Detection of Dengue Virus Lineages

Degree: MSc in Mathematical Sciences - Artificial Intelligence for Science

Institution: AIMS South Africa & Stellenbosch University

Year: 2023

This research developed an innovative machine learning pipeline for the automatic classification and early detection of Dengue virus lineages. The model achieved 97%+ accuracy and was validated against Genome Detective, making it a valuable tool for epidemiological surveillance and public health monitoring.

Key Contributions:

  • Machine learning pipeline for genomic data analysis
  • Feature extraction and selection from viral sequences
  • Model validation and comparison with existing tools
  • Implications for public health surveillance

Institution: SISSA – International School for Advanced Studies, Trieste

Year: 2025-2026
