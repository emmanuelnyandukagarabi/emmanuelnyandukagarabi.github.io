Research Interests & Projects

Advancing AI, machine learning, and data science for real-world impact

Research Focus Areas

Primary Research Area

AI for Public Health (AI4PH)

My primary research focus is on applying artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to address critical public health challenges. This includes:

  • Disease detection and prevention using ML models
  • Health outcome prediction and risk stratification
  • Population-level health interventions and epidemiological modeling
  • Healthcare system optimization and resource allocation
  • Medical decision support systems

Goal: Develop AI systems that can improve health outcomes in resource-limited settings and increase healthcare accessibility.

Core Research Area

Explainable & Interpretable AI

Understanding why AI systems make specific decisions is crucial for adoption in high-stakes domains like healthcare. My research explores:

  • SHAP values and feature importance analysis
  • LIME (Local Interpretable Model-Agnostic Explanations)
  • Attention mechanisms in neural networks
  • Feature attribution methods
  • Model transparency and trustworthiness

Goal: Create a comprehensive knowledge base for interpretable AI methods and their applications.

Research Area

Machine Learning

Development and application of advanced machine learning methodologies:

  • Supervised learning: Classification and regression
  • Unsupervised learning: Clustering and dimensionality reduction
  • Deep learning: Neural networks and convolutional architectures
  • Ensemble methods and model stacking
  • Time series analysis and forecasting
  • Natural language processing applications

Goal: Advance ML methodologies for practical scientific and healthcare applications.

Research Area

Data Management & Engineering

Robust data infrastructure and management practices for AI/ML systems:

  • Data quality assurance and validation
  • ETL pipeline design and optimization
  • Database architecture and optimization
  • Data governance and compliance
  • Big data processing and analytics
  • Data warehousing and analytics solutions

Goal: Build scalable, reliable data systems that support advanced analytics and AI applications.

Status: Ongoing | Type: Open Source Research

A comprehensive GitHub repository dedicated to exploring, implementing, and documenting methods for building interpretable and explainable AI systems. This project serves as a knowledge base for practitioners and researchers interested in understanding AI decision-making processes.

Key Components:

  • Implementations of major explainability algorithms (SHAP, LIME, etc.)

PhD Research Interests

I am particularly interested in pursuing doctoral research that combines multiple areas:

  • AI-Enabled Healthcare Systems: Developing interpretable AI systems for clinical decision support that can be deployed in real-world healthcare settings, particularly in low-resource areas.
  • Health Equity & AI4PH: Using machine learning to address health disparities and improve health outcomes for underserved populations.
  • Trustworthy AI: Creating frameworks and methodologies for building AI systems that are not only accurate but also transparent, accountable, and fair.
  • Data-Driven Public Health: Leveraging big data and advanced analytics to inform public health policy and practice.

I am actively seeking PhD programs that align with these research interests and offer opportunities for meaningful contribution to AI for social good.
